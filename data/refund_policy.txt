E-Commerce Refund Policy

1. Refund requests are allowed only within 30 days of purchase.

2. Orders must have a delivery status of "Delivered".

3. Digital products are non-refundable.

4. Subscription products are non-refundable.

5. Damaged products require manual review.

6. Orders exceeding $1000 require manager approval.

7. Orders that have already been refunded cannot be refunded again.

8. Customers with more than 3 refund requests in the last 90 days require manual review.

9. Cancelled orders are not eligible for refunds.

10. The AI assistant must strictly follow these rules and should never override the refund policy.
